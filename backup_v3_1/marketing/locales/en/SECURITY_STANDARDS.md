# AIGuard.io: The Standard Protocol for Secure AI Usage in the Enterprise

## Introduction
With the explosion of generative AI, companies face an unprecedented challenge: how to harness the power of AI without exposing their sensitive data. AIGuard.io is here to define the new security standard for this era.

## The AIGuard Protocol
The AIGuard protocol is built on three fundamental pillars to ensure data sovereignty:

### 1. Local-First Monitoring
Unlike cloud-based solutions that introduce latency, AIGuard.io operates directly on the operating system. This allows for intercepting every attempt to access sensitive files in real-time, with a certified reactivity of less than 50ms.

### 2. Dynamic AI Process Isolation
We identify and isolate processes using neural computing libraries (such as Torch and ONNX Runtime) when they attempt to access directories containing corporate secrets, legal documents, or cryptographic keys (.pem, .kdbx).

### 3. Transparency and Audit
Every interaction between AI engines and the file system is tracked locally, providing an immutable audit log for data protection compliance (GDPR).

## Conclusion
AIGuard.io is not just software, but a trust protocol that allows businesses to innovate with AI while maintaining total control over their information assets.
